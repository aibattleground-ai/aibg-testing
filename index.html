How It Works

Five simple steps from creation to victory. Your art, your glory, your winnings.

🎨

1. Create Your Art

Submit your prompt and generate AI artwork using our integrated creation tools. Each piece becomes your entry into the weekly battle.

  • → Use AIBG tokens to access generators
  • → Choose from multiple AI models
  • → Follow weekly themed challenges
🖼️

2. Enter the Arena

Your artwork is uploaded to the battle gallery where it joins other entries competing for the top spot.

  • → Instant gallery placement
  • → Public voting begins immediately
  • → Compete against best creators
🗳️

3. Community Votes

Through our Telegram bot, users participate in tournament-style 1v1 voting battles, choosing their favorites in each matchup.

  • → Ideal Type World Cup format
  • → Fair bracket-based competition
  • → Voters stake tokens on choices
👑

4. Champions Crowned

Top-voted artworks claim their position on the leaderboard. The most popular creation wins the championship.

  • → Top 3 earn champion status
  • → Rankings displayed in real-time
  • → Permanent Hall of Fame entry
💰

5. Claim Your Prize

Winners take their share of the prize pool. Voters who backed winning entries also split a portion of the winnings.

  • → Top creators receive major prizes
  • → Winning voters share pool percentage
  • → Instant token distribution
⚠️
